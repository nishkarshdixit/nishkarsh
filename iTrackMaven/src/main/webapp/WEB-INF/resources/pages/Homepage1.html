iTrack

Recruitment tracking system minimizes the efforts of the hiring managers and the human resource to keep a track of the Request Requisitions to be raised as per the project and the hiring done accordingly. Designed to allow the hiring manager and the human resource team to have the better co-ordination for the complete recruitment process.

Facilitates communication between Hiring managers, Reporting managers, BFM and the recruiters. Preformatted reply forms are used in every stage through the system to provide a uniform process. The system also contains a relational database containing the data of the request raised and the complete recruitment of the candidate from the interview to the joining date.
